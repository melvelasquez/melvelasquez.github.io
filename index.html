about me

hey there! my name is melanie velasquez (they/she), but please call me mel! i am originally from san diego, ca and currently based in the bay area. i am interested in telling stories about the intersection of culture, politics, and identity. in 2021, i graduated from the university of san francisco in media studies and then worked as a general reporting intern at the san francisco examiner. currently, i'm a first year audio and multimedia student at the uc berkeley graduate school of journalism.

what's new with mel?

may 12, 2023

i just wrote a story, produced some audio, and coded a website page for a larger project that is near and dear to my heart about our queer elders in the bay area. i am so lucky and grateful to be in one of the first generations of queer folks to have older queer folks to look up to authentically, proudly, and loudly. take a look at what i made!
